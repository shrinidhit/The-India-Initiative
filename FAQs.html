A Word With The Organizers.

Why Are You Doing This?

There are a lot of people invested in India emotionally, economically and even culturally. We found that these people are innovative and passionate but have no medium to channel their energy and ideas to make impact on home turf. This initiative is an attempt to plug that gap.

Isn't This Just Another India Conference

No.

Why?

Because you aren’t just learning or discussing ideas, you get the chance to implement some of your own alongside 49 students just as dedicated, passionate and innovative as you are.

How Do You Select The Top 50 Students?

A selection committee takes into consideration your passion towards India, your intellectual vitality and desire to take action.

How Will We Implement Our Ideas In India If We're Not There?

We partner with local organizations across the country in various fields that have operations and movements and can work off your proposed solutions. In other words, if you can create a practical strategy, you’ll have the opportunity to see it in action.

Who Is Eligible To Apply?

Any undergraduate studying in the United States is welcome to apply.
If you’re a business professional, we have room for you too. Contact us and we will be in touch with you.

Do I Need To Be Indian To Apply?

Of course not. You need to be passionate about implementing change towards the advancement of India.

What Is The Structure

A conference where those chosen get to participate and have a dialogue in round-table discussion by experts in a plethora of topics, listen and converse with renowned speakers, and to implement ideas that stem from the conference.

What Is The Timeframe?

In late summer, we will release the applications for the conference and the incubator. In early Fall, we will send out acceptances and have opportunities for selected candidates to introduce themselves, network and share ideas. The 50 selected candidates will then meet in New York City for two days and listen to renowned speakers and network with top leaders in their respective fields.

What Happens After The Conference?

You have 49 passionate individuals that you share a network with. You also get to work with Indian organizations and implement your ideas.

How Much Do I Pay?

We are currently working with our partners to get the exact numbers but we expect to provide funding for travel and lodging.
